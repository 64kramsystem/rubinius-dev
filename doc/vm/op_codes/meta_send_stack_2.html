meta_send_stack_2

Byte code: 0x4b

Operation

Optimised send for performing a method taking two parameters

Format

meta_send_stack_2 mthd

Stack

Before After

Description

Pops the receiver recv off the top of the stack, and sends it the method identified by mthd. The two parameters value1 and value2 to be used by the method are left on the top of the stack.
