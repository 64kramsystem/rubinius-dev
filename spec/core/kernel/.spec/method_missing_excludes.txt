Kernel#method_missing is not called when a defined method is called
Kernel#method_missing is called when an undefined method is called
Kernel#method_missing is called when a private method is called
When Kernel#method_missing is undefined a NoMethodError is raised when an undefined method is called
When Kernel#method_missing is undefined a NoMethodError is raised when a private method is called
When Kernel#method_missing is undefined a NoMethodError is raised when a protected method is called
